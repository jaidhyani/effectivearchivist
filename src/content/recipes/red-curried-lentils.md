instructions:
  - type: "step"
    text: "Place the lentils in a large pot and add 5 cups water. Turn heat to high and bring to a boil."
  - type: "step"
    text: "After the lentils begin to boil, reduce heat to low-medium and simmer for about 20 minutes, uncovered, stirring roughly every five minutes. Add more water if needed - the lentils are done when they are tender (but not mushy) and most of the water is absorbed. Drain the remaining liquid."
  - type: "step"
    text: "While the lentils cook, make the sauce: dice the onion (if using raw onion). Mince/press the garlic cloves (if using raw garlic)."
  - type: "step"
    text: "Measure and combine the dried spices in a small bowl: garam masala, curry powder, turmeric, ground ginger, onion powder/granules (if using), garlic powder/granules (if using)."
  - type: "step"
    text: "Heat the olive oil or butter in a large saucepan over medium high heat. If using raw onion, add it to the pan and saute for approximately ten minutes."
  - type: "step"
    text: "Add the combined spices and stir for approximately a minute."
  - type: "step"
    text: "Add the red curry paste, tomato paste, and sweetener (honey, agave, or sugar) and stir to combine."
  - type: "step"
    text: "Add the coconut milk and stir until the sauce is smooth."
  - type: "step"
    text: "Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook until the sauce reaches serving temperature. If the lentils are running late, stir occasionally and maybe reduce the heat to low. Add the cooked lentils and serve."
